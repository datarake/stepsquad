Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2, 2025

StepSquad ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service at stepsquad.club.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Email address, password (hashed), display name
  • Step Data: Daily step counts, dates, competition participation
  • Team Information: Team names, team memberships
  • Competition Data: Participation in competitions, rankings
  • Profile Information: Any additional profile information you choose to provide

1.2 Information from Fitness Devices

When you connect your fitness device (Garmin, Fitbit) to StepSquad, we collect:

  • OAuth Tokens: Access tokens and refresh tokens for device authentication
  • Step Data: Daily step counts synced from your device
  • Sync Metadata: Timestamps, device information, sync status

Note: We only access step data. We do not collect other health or fitness data (heart rate, sleep, etc.) unless explicitly authorized by you.

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

When you use StepSquad, we automatically collect:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent on the Service
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type
  • Log Data: IP addresses, access times, error logs
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: Session cookies, authentication tokens

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and Improve the Service: Operate StepSquad, process competitions, display leaderboards
  • Sync Step Data: Retrieve step counts from connected fitness devices
  • Authenticate Users: Verify user identity, manage accounts, secure access
  • Communicate with You: Send service updates, competition notifications, respond to inquiries
  • Ensure Security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, unauthorized access
  • Comply with Legal Obligations: Meet legal requirements, respond to legal requests
  • Analytics: Understand usage patterns, improve user experience (aggregated, anonymized data)

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

3.1 Public Competition Data

The following information is visible to other users in competitions:

  • Team names and team memberships
  • Step counts and rankings (in the context of competitions)
  • Leaderboard positions

3.2 Service Providers

We may share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, including:

  • Cloud Hosting: Google Cloud Platform (Firestore, Cloud Run)
  • Authentication: Firebase Authentication
  • Fitness Device APIs: Garmin Connect API, Fitbit Web API (for syncing step data)

These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests, such as:

  • Court orders, subpoenas, or other legal processes
  • Government investigations or law enforcement requests
  • Protecting the rights, property, or safety of StepSquad, our users, or others

3.4 Business Transfers

If StepSquad is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change in ownership.

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Data transmitted over the internet is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Secure Storage: User data and OAuth tokens are stored securely in Google Firestore
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Authentication: Secure password hashing and token-based authentication
  • Regular Security Updates: We keep our systems updated with security patches

Note: No method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

5.1 Access and Portability

You can:

  • Access your account information at any time through your account settings
  • View your step history and competition data
  • Export your data (contact us for assistance)

5.2 Correction and Updates

You can update or correct your account information at any time through your account settings.

5.3 Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated data by:

  • Using the account deletion feature in your settings, or
  • Contacting us at support@stepsquad.club

Note: Some information may be retained as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

5.4 Device Disconnection

You can disconnect your fitness device at any time through your device settings. This will:

  • Revoke StepSquad's access to your device data
  • Delete stored OAuth tokens
  • Stop automatic step syncing

Historical step data already submitted to competitions may be retained.

5.5 Opt-Out Rights

You can:

  • Opt out of marketing emails by clicking "unsubscribe" in email communications
  • Disable cookies in your browser settings (may affect Service functionality)

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

StepSquad uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Authentication: Maintain your login session
  • Preferences: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Analytics: Understand how users interact with the Service (aggregated data)

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling cookies may affect Service functionality.

7. Children's Privacy

StepSquad is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at support@stepsquad.club, and we will delete such information.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country of residence. By using StepSquad, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries that may have different data protection laws than your country.

9. Third-Party Services

StepSquad integrates with third-party services:

  • Garmin Connect: For syncing step data from Garmin devices
  • Fitbit Web API: For syncing step data from Fitbit devices
  • Firebase Authentication: For user authentication
  • Google Cloud Platform: For hosting and data storage

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their privacy policies:

  • Garmin Privacy Policy
  • Fitbit Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Cloud Privacy Policy

10. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as:

  • Your account is active, or
  • As needed to provide you services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements

When you delete your account, we will:

  • Delete your account information and personal data
  • Retain anonymized, aggregated data that cannot identify you
  • Retain data as required by law or for legitimate business purposes

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting the updated Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending email notifications for significant changes

Your continued use of the Service after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@stepsquad.club
  • Website: https://stepsquad.club

13. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including:

  • The right to know what personal information is collected, used, shared, or sold
  • The right to delete personal information
  • The right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (StepSquad does not sell personal information)
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@stepsquad.club.

14. GDPR Rights (EU Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including:

  • The right to access your personal data
  • The right to rectify inaccurate data
  • The right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • The right to restrict processing
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to object to processing
  • The right to withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@stepsquad.club.
